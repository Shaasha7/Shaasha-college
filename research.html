Research & Development Cell

Research and Development Cell (R&D Cell) is established to promote research culture among faculty and students by creating an environment that encourages innovation, consultancy, entrepreneurship and academic excellence. The cell supports research projects, publications, patents, conferences, workshops and industrial collaborations.

Core Values

Research & Inquiry
Professionalism & Ethics
Integrity & Transparency
Entrepreneurial & Innovative Team Work

Objectives

To gather collective efforts from every department, faculty and stakeholders to enhance institutional research performance and improve NIRF Ranking.
To motivate faculty members for higher education, research publications, funded projects, consultancy and patent filing.
To promote research culture and scholarly activities among faculty and students through seminars, workshops and conferences.
To encourage innovation, incubation and entrepreneurship through industry collaboration.

Activities

National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF)
Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) & Technology Transfer
Industrial Institutional Linkage (IIL)
International Conferences, Workshops & Seminars
Incubation & Start-Ups
