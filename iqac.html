About IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) is an important administrative body responsible for planning, guiding and monitoring Quality Assurance and Quality Enhancement activities of the institution. The IQAC works towards realizing the goals of quality enhancement and sustenance. Its main responsibility is to develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic improvement in the academic and administrative performance of the institution. The IQAC acts as a facilitative and participative unit rather than another hierarchical structure. It promotes a culture of quality among faculty, students and stakeholders.

The success of IQAC depends on participation from every stakeholder in the institution. It plans intervention strategies, promotes best practices, documents quality initiatives and monitors institutional performance. IQAC encourages innovation in teaching-learning, research, extension activities and institutional governance. Its objective is to ensure continuous improvement in every aspect of the college, making quality a defining element of higher education.

To become a center of excellence through continuous quality enhancement, innovation, research, academic leadership and societal contribution.
