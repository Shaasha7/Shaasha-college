INSTRUCTIONS TO CANDIDATES

  1. The Hall Ticket must be preserved safely until the completion of the End Semester Examinations. Candidates will not be permitted to enter the Examination Hall without a valid Hall Ticket and College ID Card.
  2. Candidates shall produce the Hall Ticket and College ID Card on demand by the Hall Superintendent, Chief Superintendent, or any other competent authority.
  3. Admission to the examination is provisional.
  4. The Hall Ticket is issued subject to the candidate fulfilling attendance and other requirements as per the regulations of the Institution.
  5. Candidates will not be allowed to enter the Examination Hall after 30 minutes from the commencement of the examination and shall not leave before the expiry of 30 minutes.
  6. Candidates shall occupy only the seat allotted to them as per the seating arrangement indicated in the Hall Ticket.
  7. Candidates must write their Register Number on the Question Paper immediately upon receipt.
  8. Candidates must verify the correctness of the Question Paper (course code, title, and number of pages).
  9. Candidates shall bring their own pens, pencils, erasers, and other permitted stationery.
  10. Exchange of calculators or any materials inside the Examination Hall is prohibited. Only non-programmable calculators are permitted.
  11. The use of programmable calculators, mobile phones, smart watches, laptops, digital diaries, or any electronic gadgets is strictly prohibited.
  12. Candidates shall not make any marks or write anything in the Answer Book that could reveal their identity.
  13. Candidates shall not detach any page from the Answer Book.
  14. Candidates must verify that the Hall Superintendent has affixed the correct barcode on the Answer Book.
  15. All rough work must be done only in the Answer Book.
  16. Candidates must maintain strict silence and discipline throughout the examination.
  17. Candidates must sign the attendance sheet and verify their details.
  18. Candidates must hand over the Answer Script personally before leaving the Examination Hall.
  19. Candidates found using unfair means will be booked under malpractice.
  20. Any form of impersonation will be treated as a serious offence.
  21. Candidates suffering from infectious diseases must inform the Chief Superintendent in advance.
  22. Candidates must keep their belongings only in the designated area.
  23. Only approved log tables or data sheets (if applicable) are permitted.
