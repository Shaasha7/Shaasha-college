About NAAC

The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) is an autonomous institution established by the University Grants Commission (UGC) to assess and accredit Higher Educational Institutions in India. Shaasha College of Technology is committed to maintaining excellence in teaching, research, innovation, governance and student support.
