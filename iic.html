About IIC

Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India has established the Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) to systematically foster the culture of innovation among Higher Education Institutions. The primary objective of IIC is to encourage, inspire and nurture young students by supporting them to work with innovative ideas and transform them into successful prototypes. Shaasha College of Engineering (SCE) focuses on creating a vibrant atmosphere for students by organizing seminars, workshops, innovation challenges, hackathons, codathons, makeathons, design competitions and datathons. This innovation ecosystem enables students to develop creative ideas and transform them into successful prototypes and startup ventures.
